What this is. A path-level Monte Carlo for SVC. Spot evolves under Heston stochastic vol with Merton jumps; options are priced and Greeks computed via Black-Scholes off your entry IV, held constant across the path. Outputs are framed against your $10k reference account and management points: 50% credit target, 21 DTE, expiry.

What this isn't. An oracle. Constant IV means the sim leans on theta and spot behavior and will understate the edge of selling elevated IV (no crush modeled). Results describe the shape of outcomes under your assumptions, not a forecast. Not investment advice.
